rule

The No-Line Rule

Designers are prohibited from using 1px solid borders for sectioning. Boundaries must be defined through background color shifts or vertical space (16–20 spacing units).

Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

check_circle

Do

  • ✓Use asymmetrical spacing — allow more white space on the right side of containers for editorial flow.
  • ✓Use headline-lg for card titles to give an authoritative, newspaper-header feel.
  • ✓Use primary-fixed as background for highlight cards to draw the eye to high-yield opportunities.
  • ✓Use background color shifts or vertical space (16–20 spacing units) to separate sections.
  • ✓Use 0px 12px 32px rgba(26,28,28,0.06) for ambient shadow on floating elements.
  • ✓Use Glassmorphism (85% opacity + 12px backdrop-blur) for sticky navigation elements.
cancel

Don't

  • ✗Use 1px dividers or lines to separate list items. Use 24px of vertical space or a background shift instead.
  • ✗Use pure black #000000. Always use on-background #1a1c1c for text.
  • ✗Use high-intensity shadows. If it looks like a shadow, it's too dark — it should feel like a "soft glow of absence."
  • ✗Use DEFAULT or sm roundedness for large components. Use lg or xl.
  • ✗Move cards "up" via shadow on hover — transition the background color to surface-bright instead.
  • ✗Use 100% opaque borders. If accessibility requires a border, use outline-variant at 15% opacity only.
